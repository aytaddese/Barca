FC Barcelona

Basic Information

The FCB teams slogan is “Mes que un club” which means in English More than a club. The slogan was put into use by the founder of the club Joan Gamper. In my opinion, the slogan means that not only is FC Barcelona a club, but it is also a source of emotion.What I mean is that when Real Madrid scores in El Classico, the fans sometimes start crying. The Football teams stadium is known as Camp Nou. The president is Josep Maria Bartomeu, while the first team is coached by Ernesto Valverde. The president has a different role because as a president of the club, he has to look at economic problems and also has the responsibility to fire any player or coach because of a certain reason. However, the coach of the team has strategic problems. Founded on November 29, 1899, Barca plays in La Liga, Copa del Rey, Champions League, Europa League, Supercopa de España, Supercopa de Catalunya, International Cup, and the Joan Gamper.La Liga is the local Spanish league. The Copa del Rey is a national competition including all La Liga participants and the first twelve teams of the second division of La Liga. The Champions League is a national league run by UEFA. The first one to four teams of every European national team makes it to this major competition. The Europa league is also managed by UEFA, and it is for the two teams that miss a Champions League spot. The other half of the teams are from players who get eliminated in the group stage of the Champion League. The Supercopa de España is runned by the Spanish National Soccer Federation. The participants are the winner of La Liga andthe winner of the Copa del Rey. The International Cup is a pre-season tournament for major teams across the world. The Joan Gamper is the opening of the stadium every year. Barca plays against a team chosen by the management team. FCB’s biggest competitor is Real Madrid.

Cant del Barca

The anthem is an important part of a Barca game experience in Camp Nou. The players start walking onto the pitch and stay there. The fans rise from their seats. The energy flows through to pitch. You can hear the chants of the fans, filled with proudness of being a Barca fan. The song continues loudly, going with lots of importance to the fans of the team. You can see the players hand on their heart. Finally, you can enjoy the silence in Camp Nou. Suddenly, the players lift their hands and start clapping. Then, the players walk through the lines high fiving each other. After high fiving the whole other team, the captains go the referee to decide who starts and the players take their place in the field. The ball is touched, and a game full of emotion awaits the fans. Since 2008-09 el Cant del Barça has been the official anthem of Barcelona. The Cant del Barca is always sung in Catalan because the team is a Catalan team.
